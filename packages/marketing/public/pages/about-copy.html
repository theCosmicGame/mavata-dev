About

We level the playing field for private companies.

Successful sellers in today’s M&A world share two traits:

1.Value-Driving Data: They regularly track & centralize the business metrics that drive sale value.
‍
2. Market Knowledge: They actively monitor valuation & buyer trends.

Private Equity firms knows this – it’s partly why they require monthly reporting and frequent valuations on their companies.  
‍
Business owners should have access to the same reporting and value monitoring capabilities. Nobody should sell undervalue and overpay on fees because of asymmetric information.  
‍
The future is now here. 
‍
Mavata is preparing shareholders to succeed in the modern world. 
‍
By blending monthly data centralization with anonymous access to buyers, we are able to confidentially preview & monitor valuation, optimize readiness, and influence the ability for private companies to earn maximum shareholder value for their business. 
‍
Mavata makes it easy. 

Our Mission

Our mission is to make M&A transparent, efficient, and accessible for private companies.

Our Values

Transparency. Efficiency. Fairness.

Our Background

Mavata was founded by a former award-winning M&A dealmaker.

As a senior member of one of New York’s most established M&A advisory firms for private companies, our founder witnessed firsthand how costly and inefficient the sale process can be for underprepared private companies. Our goal is to change this - every private company should have the information and access necessary to maximize shareholder value.

Our Clients

We work with companies that are considering a sale within the next five years – often within the next two.

Our clients invest in our monthly subscription service to save on future advisor fees and have confidence in the value of their business.

We specialize in serving companies in the “lower middle market” – in other words, companies with revenues between $5 million and $150 million.
